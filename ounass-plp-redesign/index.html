Curated For You
Reinvented

Five takes on the Curated For You browse surface. Each pushes beyond the 2-up grid toward a distinct browsing model. Open any prototype on mobile to experience the full interaction.

01 · Story Scroll — Single-item-per-screen vertical swipe, with the "why it was curated" reason promoted into a visible chip. Treats browsing like flipping through a zine; reduces decision fatigue for the highest-intent curated surface.

02 · Dense Discovery — 3-up tight grid with inline quick-add, color swatches, and a sticky chip rail. ~50% more items visible per screen. Best when the goal is fast comparison and refinement.

03 · Swipe Stack — Curated cards surfaced one at a time. Swipe right saves, left passes, up opens details, down quick-adds. A Tinder-for-luxury model — ideal for the daily-edit moment.

04 · Magazine — Asymmetric hero + thumbs rhythm with an editorial quote strip between rows. Communicates luxury and curatorial voice while keeping density higher than the current 2-up.

05 · Masonry — Pinterest-style two-column staggered grid with varied tile heights. Minimal meta lets the image do the work — best for serendipitous discovery across many items.
